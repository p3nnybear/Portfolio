Born in Taiwan and raised in United states.

Passionate about inclusive design, content-centered experiences, hospitality design and fashion.



I am a designer who aspires to bring change through intuitive composition and delightful experiences. The process of problem-solving has always been innate for me. I am energized by a problem’s complexities. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work in different industries and partake in unique roles, and I believe my diverse experience has made me a humble person who is equipped with skills that are crucial for a designer. As an effective communicator and an empathetic problem solver, I am passionate about speaking the user’s language and creating solutions that address their pain points. My background in interior architecture and design has taught me the importance of design pricipal and its key drivers for success; allowing me to achieve harmony by building products with both the user and company goals in mind. I came to United States in the age of 16 as an exchange student. After graduation from University, I started as a interior designer at world-famous hospitality design firm. I was introduced to many different aspects and quickly learned the design principles and skills to provide client success in projects. I have always interested in technology and continually learning and adapting to new products in the fast-pace industry. We get to see design everywhere in our life, the design can make the human condition more convenient. On the other hand, technology plays such a large role in life. I want to be more well-rounded, designing the interior to technology. As time went on, I want to become a front-end developer by learning HTML,CSS and JavaScript. I want to make sure all the users can use the products that they are not familiar with. I’ve seen the true value of design, it allows me to create our best ideas through web/app development. It’s a great tool that has made me into a full-fledged professional and an entrepreneur. I look forward to joining a team that will lead and shape the future of experience design. “I fill Space With Imagination.”—LeRoy Winbush
